Some Fun Facts!

India won the gold medal in Field Hockey six Olympic Games in a row between 1928 and 1956.

South Korea has won 27 out of the 39 gold medals distributed for Archery since 1984.

Despite only participating in nine Olympic Games, the USSR has the second highest number of Olympic medals. The USA, which has the most Olympic medals, has participated in 28 Olympic Games.
